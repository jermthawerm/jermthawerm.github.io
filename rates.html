Overnight Rates

  • Effective January 1, 2024, base rates are $60 - $75 per night plus tax (both peak season and off peak season)
  • Winter monthly rates available - please call to inquire.
  • The base rate is for one site and includes:
    • 4 adults, or a family of 2 adults with up to 4 children under 18
    • One vehicle
    • GENERATOR HOURS ARE 10 AM - 6 PM (STRICTLY ENFORCED)
  • Additional charges apply for additional persons and vehicles as listed below.

Vehicle Limit

  • No more than one vehicle per site
  • No more than one RV or Camper per site
  • Please note that not all sites are the same size, and there may not be enough room on some sites for a second vehicle or for multiple tents. Please discuss your needs when you call for reservations.

Check In After 2 PM

  • Your reservation begins at 2 PM on the arrival date.
  • You may arrive anytime after 2 PM on your arrival date.
  • Early arrivals are subject to space available and additional fees.
  • If you have not arrived by 1 PM the day after your arrival date, and we have not heard from you, your reservation will be cancelled.

Check Out Before 1 PM

  • Checkout time is 1 PM on the last day of your reservation.
  • Checking out after this time must be arranged in advance to avoid conflicts with incoming campers and is subject to additional fees.

Pets

  • Pets must be leashed at all times in the campground per county ordinance.
  • Pets must not be left unattended.
  • Pets must be cleaned up after, especially on the beach. All state and county regulations apply.
  • Parvo and Rabies vaccination certificates should be available.
  • Dangerous dogs are not allowed.

Payment Information, Refunds & Changes

  • A Visa, MasterCard, Discover or American Express credit card is required to make and confirm your reservation.
  • There is a $10 non-refundable processing fee to make a reservation.
  • For stays of one week or less, you will be charged the processing fee plus the first night's stay when making your reservation.
  • For longer stays, prepayment of one night per week is required when making your reservation (for example, a three week stay will require 3 days prepaid).
  • Payment of all remaining fees is due prior to occupying the camping site(s).
  • The total amount due upon arrival can be paid by Credit Card, Cash, or Check.
  • There is a $10.00 cancellation fee per site. This will apply to each reservation/site.
  • Reservations, cancellations, or changes to a reservation are only accepted by phone or voicemail message.
  • Reservations, cancellations, or changes to a reservation are not accepted by email.
  • Cancellation less than 30 days in advance will result in forfeiture of deposit.
Please note that rates are subject to change without notice.
